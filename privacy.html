Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2, 2026

At RP Asset Integrity, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us through our website forms, as well as data collected automatically through your interaction with our site.

Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, and phone number.

Usage Data: Information on how you access and use the website, including your IP address, browser type, and pages visited.

2. Cookies and Analytics

Our website uses cookies, which are small text files placed on your device, to help us analyse how visitors use our site.

Analytics: We use third party tools such as Google Analytics to track website traffic and user behaviour. This helps us improve our website performance and user experience.

Control: You can set your browser to refuse cookies, although some features of our site may not function properly without them.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the data we collect for the following purposes:

Callback Service: To contact you by phone or email as requested to discuss our services.

Marketing and Promotions: To send you updates, newsletters, and promotional offers to help you get the most out of our services.

Client Acquisition: To understand your needs and offer tailored solutions to bring you on as a valued client.

Improvement: To monitor trends and improve the content and functionality of our website.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. We may only share information with trusted third party service providers who assist us in operating our website or conducting our business, such as our email marketing platform, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

5. Your Opt Out Rights

You are in control of your data.

Emails: You can unsubscribe from our marketing list at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email we send.

Data Access: You may contact us at any time to request a copy of your data or ask that we delete your information from our database.

6. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you enter, submit, or access your data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy at any time. When we do, we will revise the effective date at the top of this page.
